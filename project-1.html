Nought | Zero-Proof Cocktails

N0UGHT’s packaging is infused with one succinct and succulent concept; visual taste. We started by asking ourselves, “How do you show the experience of a flavour through design?” For an innovation-focused brand, that meant picking abstraction over actuality. Let’s reward the customers who notice the details.

From there, we crafted a set of eleven flavour symbols that build a distinct “palate pattern.” Each drink offers its own unique sensory experience through — you guessed it — visuals and taste, e.g., lime green = lime flavour. Mind-blowing, right?

Reminiscent of N0UGHT’s dynamic patrons, we wanted our take-home cocktails to feel just as vibrant. Pairing our flavours with bright, monochromatic hues that stand out against black only made sense. We heightened the liveliness with a contrasting type scale, using bold headers of similar visual interest to the pattern and subtler body copy for variety and eye rest. And to tie it all together, the drink’s description exudes deliciousness, affirming the choice to take a sip before you start drooling.
